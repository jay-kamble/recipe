Ingredients

  • 500g mutton, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 large onion, finely sliced
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 4-5 green chilies, slit
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as required

Instructions

  1. Heat ghee or oil in a large pot. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Cook for a minute.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft.
  4. Add mutton pieces and cook until they are browned.
  5. Add yogurt and biryani masala. Mix well and cook for a few minutes.
  6. Add coriander leaves, mint leaves, and salt. Stir and cook until the mutton is tender.
  7. In a separate pot, cook basmati rice until it’s 70% done.
  8. Layer the partially cooked rice over the mutton. Cover and cook on low heat until the rice is fully cooked.
  9. Garnish with fried onions and serve hot.
