Ingredients

  • 1 cup matki (moong beans), soaked overnight
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp Misal masala
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 1 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Pav (bread rolls) for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add chopped onions. Sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
  4. Add Misal masala and cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add soaked matki and mix well. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Add grated coconut, tamarind paste, and salt. Stir and cook for another 10 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  8. Serve hot with Pav (bread rolls).
