Ingredients

  • 1 cup mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, beans, peas)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 large tomato, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 2 tbsp Pav Bhaji masala
  • 1 tbsp cumin seeds
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Pav (bread rolls) for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add onions and green chilies. Sauté until onions turn golden brown.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
  3. Add Pav Bhaji masala and cook for a few minutes until the spices are well combined.
  4. Add the mixed vegetables and mashed potatoes. Mix well and cook for another 5-7 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and cook until everything is well combined and heated through.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  7. Serve hot with Pav (bread rolls) toasted with butter.
